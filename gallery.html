Gravitas (Mockup Project)

Gravitas is an e-commerce platform specializing in high-performance computers and laptops designed for gamers, professionals, and power users. With a focus on speed, reliability, and cutting-edge technology. Gravitas offers a curated selection of gaming laptops, workstations, custom-built systems, and premium accessories. The site ensures a seamless shopping experience with secure payments, fast delivery, and expert support for personalized advice and technical assistance.

Roomtual (Room Designer Prototype)

Roomtual is a room designer application prototype that empowers users to design their spaces with ease. It features a user-friendly interface for creating custom room layouts and includes a convenient option to order furniture directly through the app. The goal is to provide a seamless experience, combining creativity and functionality, to bring room designs to life.
